Migration to Github Pages

2015-12-29 |

Today I am migrating all of my blog post from blogger.com to Github Pages. And this decision is purely from my urge as a hacker, or a developer, whatever they call me.

With this migration, I wish to myself to be more productive in writing, and also coding. I push myself at 02: xx pm to create this post.

I am sure, with GitHub pages, the thing is more clearly than previously. I hope I will be more productive as I expect to be. But, I still struggle to finish my personal project, though.
