Block any social media distractions

2016-02-07 |

It has been two weeks long (maybe three), I’ve stopped completely all of my social networking activities that cause me unproductive. All of the procrastination that I did it before, many of them is because of scrolling down, read all my friends’ status -many of them is not important at all. Yea, I’ve blocked all social media distractions. Especially from Facebook.

Facebook is social media which has the most contribution bring me up mentally unproductive. Every single day, I spend all of my time to refresh my Facebook’s newsfeed. Scrolling to the bottom, and then refresh newsfeed again and again. Repetitive until I feel not enough time to do something productive such as coding -I am a programmer, though.

At the beginning when I made this decision, I only logged out Facebook desktop via the browser. The app in smartphone still often ruin my focus and productivity. Every time it notifies me, or when I am rest, I always still open the app.

A few days later, I am not familiar with this situation at all. I often feel bored when I’m in rest. But, this feeling is temporary. A few days later after it, I experienced the weird feeling.

The weird feeling is I am disconnected from the world -the internet. Can you imagine? Every I see newsfeed besides all statuses of all my friends that unimportant at all, I use it to read science news, the world news and interesting content from several media.

However, such feeling is disappeared a few days later. From feel disconnected out of the entire world to feel freedom. Free in the sense that I am no longer disturbed with those annoying statuses from my friendslist, that always enters into my mind even still echoed a few hours later.

Pros and cons

Apart from that, Facebook has a lot of pros mentally, such as feel connected to the world -a.ka internet, and have new knowledge by subscribing science and world news from Facebook fanspage. It also has a lot of bad things which may harm me mentally, for the next hours to a few days after I read those annoying statuses.

For me living in Indonesia, as Indonesian -which everyone here always overdramatic, I always feel annoyed by those statuses that show off their wealthy, something that maybe others does not have, or it’s very difficult to achieve by others. I realize that each person has a different way to achieve something, they have their own roadmap, but show off their achievement excessively, it’s like mocking other people that they can’t do it. Late to do it, etc.

It’s nothing to do with jealousy, it’s about demotivation with inelegantly way. I did not support statuses about motivation, but I do not respect more with demotivation in a not elegantly way, it’s too tacky. So jealousy and do not like with demotivation in a not elegantly way. But for ignorant people, this seems the same.

In addition, getting annoyed by statuses such that, I also annoyed by bigot-like statuses. Particularly about religion issue and social issue. I realize that this is repetitive every year. From new year’s eve issue, other religious holiday issues (because here the only religion is I**, another religion is not important and bad from their perspective), valentine’s day, etc.

Feeling annoyed like this is not the only one of the factor to disconnect from a social network, until an unspecified time. Maybe one day I will create a new advertising campaign in Facebook, I will login again as an advertiser rather than a normal user. In addition, getting annoyed by those, take advantage of the time being spent on social media activities up to productive thing is my main priority.

Finally, I realize that pursuing my dream while doing social activities virtually is very tiring and should be stopped immediately, unless there is something important thing to do it, for a long term.

From now on, I will invest my precious time in something productive such as programming.

Ok, what’s the next challenge? I am ready to do it.
